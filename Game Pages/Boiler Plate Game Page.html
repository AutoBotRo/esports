GUILTY GEAR STRIVE

Guilty Gear Strive Box Art

Guilty Gear Strive is a fast-paced 2D fighting game released in June 2021. It features stunning 3D cel-shaded graphics and introduces new gameplay mechanics like the "Roman Cancel" system and "Dust" attacks. The game continues the story of the Guilty Gear series, with a focus on a conflict between various characters and factions. It offers a diverse roster of characters, each with unique abilities and playstyles. With a single-player story mode, arcade mode, versus mode, and online multiplayer, Guilty Gear Strive provides engaging gameplay for both newcomers and experienced players. It has received positive reviews for its accessibility, visuals, and depth.
